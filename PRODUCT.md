# Product

## Register
product

## Objective
To deliver the most precise, data-driven analytical terminal for the FIFA World Cup 2026. The platform bridges the gap between raw statistical modeling (ELO, Poisson, Machine Learning) and actionable betting intelligence, rewarding methodical users with high-fidelity insights.

## Users
- **High-Stakes Bettors**: Individuals looking for algorithmic edges and market inefficiencies.
- **Tournament Enthusiasts**: Fans seeking deep tactical trends and probabilistic knockout paths.
- **Data Analysts**: Users who value transparency in model weights and xG breakdowns.

## Core Features

### Group Stage Analysis
- **Standings Simulator**: Real-time group standings driven by Monte Carlo simulations.
- **Match Probabilities**: Deep xG breakdowns for every group fixture, including host nation advantages.

### Value Bets
- **Market Edge Detection**: Automated identification of value in O/U, BTTS, and 1X2 markets vs bookmaker lines.
- **Kelly Criterion Sizing**: Suggested stake percentages based on model confidence and bankroll management.

### Market Strategies
- **90% Ultra-Safe / 80% High-Value**: Pre-calculated accumulator strategies based on cumulative qualification probabilities.

### Historical Validation
- **Backtesting**: Transparent model performance metrics based on World Cup 2022 results, including Log-Loss and Accuracy benchmarks.

## Brand Personality
Premium, Airy, and Methodical. The interface should feel like a native Apple application—clean, spacious, and extremely polished. It rewards the user with high-fidelity data presented through an elegant, non-intimidating lens.

## Anti-references
- **Tactical Slop**: Avoid "military-grade" or "hacker" aesthetics (neon on black).
- **Dense Friction**: No tracking-heavy condensed fonts or cluttered, margin-less layouts.
- **Cheap Gamification**: Avoid loud "winner" animations or betting-cliché "fire" icons.

## Design Principles
- **Precision over Decoration**: Every pixel must serve the data.
- **Data is the UI**: Use the information itself to create hierarchy.
- **Expert Confidence**: Provide density without chaos; the tool should feel like a professional instrument.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA contrast for all critical data.
- Full keyboard navigability for the analytical terminal.
- Semantic HTML for screen reader compatibility with complex data tables.
