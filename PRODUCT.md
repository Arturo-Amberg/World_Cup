# Product

## Register

product

## Users
High-stakes bettors, World Cup data enthusiasts, and tactical analysts who require precise, data-driven match predictions and betting insights.

## Product Purpose
To provide a high-fidelity analytical terminal for World Cup 2026, enabling users to identify value bets and tournament trends through advanced modeling (ELO, Poisson, ML).

## Brand Personality
Premium, Airy, and Methodical. The interface should feel like a native Apple application—clean, spacious, and extremely polished. It rewards the user with high-fidelity data presented through an elegant, non-intimidating lens.

## Anti-references
- Harsh, military-style "tactical terminal" aesthetics.
- Pure black backgrounds with neon high-contrast accents.
- Dense, tracking-heavy condensed typography.
- Sharp, 0px border-radius corners.

## Design Principles
- **Precision over Decoration**: Every visual element must serve the data. Avoid purely decorative elements that distract from the analysis.
- **Data is the UI**: Use the information itself to create hierarchy and structure.
- **Expert Confidence**: The UI should feel reliable and authoritative, rewarding power users with density while remaining logically organized.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA contrast standards for critical data.
- Clear focus indicators for keyboard-driven analysis.
- High readability for dense tables.
